Registration

*Brunswick Eruption 11 will take place on November 3rd, 2012*

FTC teams interested in participating in the practice day scrimmage at this year's Brunswick Eruption should e-mail Vince Frascella at vincent.frascella@baesystems.com.

September 19th Update:
Registration for Brunswick Eruption 11 is full! Any teams that register will be placed on the waiting list and will be invited to compete on a first-come, first-served basis if any slots open up.

The fee to register your FRC team is $200. If you bring a pre-rookie along (and lend them a robot to compete with), you get a $100 discount, which means you only have to pay $100. If you bring two pre-rookies, your registration is free. All pre-rookies get in for free. Payment details as follows:

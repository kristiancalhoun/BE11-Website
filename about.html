About Brunswick Eruption

Brunswick Eruption is a FIRST robotics off-season event hosted by Team 25, Raider Robotix. The goal of the FIRST Robotics Competition is to get students involved in the fields of science and engineering by participating in robotics competitions. Brunswick Eruption (BE) was started in 2002 mainly as a way to get more FRC teams active in the region. Today our goals still remain the same. Usually over 40 teams from all over the northeast participate, some coming from as far away as Canada. This year, the Brunswick Eruption competition is a replay of the 2012 FIRST Rebound Rumble game.

About RINOS

Rookies In Need Of Support (RINOS) is a program started by Team 25. RINOS pairs up rookie teams with experienced veteran teams in order to help them through their first season.

This radical new concept evolved when a team from Baldwin, NY participated in Brunswick Eruption in 2004. They came to Brunswick Eruption before they were an official FIRST team by borrowing a neighboring team's robot. After seeing this, we thought that this is the best way to go ahead and start new teams because they will be experienced and excited before their first season. Since then, we have been re-fitting old robots to play the current game and letting pre-rookie teams compete with them. We also encourage all veteran teams capable of doing so to do so.

About Project Empower

Project Empower is an organization dedicated to education revitalization. Their mission is to promote life readiness skills to students across the globe. Through event-based learning competitions, they help students to discover their potential, live their passions, and own their futures. Project Empower works closely with partner organizations, companies, and investors to deliver new business and learning initiatives to students of all ages. Visit the Project Empower Website to learn more about this amazing organization.

Also check out the article about Project Empower in the NY Daily News!

Project Empower at Brunswick Eruption:

Hoku Award Competition:

Small groups of students from each FRC team will be asked to present their team's five-minute pitch to a panel of Project Empower judges, who will be role-playing as potential investors/sponsors. The judges will provide feedback to teams on their business analysis as well as ways they can improve their robotics pitch and intertwine business principles within their already established engineering ones. Project Empower will award the winning team with a $1000 sponsorship, and also mentor them for a year on how to best implement their business strategies to promote a sustainable team.

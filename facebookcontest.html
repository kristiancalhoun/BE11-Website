Facebook Photo Contest

What is your team doing in the off-season to prepare for Brunswick Eruption? Training new drivers? Adding new robot functionality? Catching some sun? Whatever it is, we want to see it!

Send us a picture of how your team is getting ready to compete at BE and you'll earn your team 50 Aloha Award points. The fan-favorite photo will win 250 points!

Rules
  1. Always practice safety first.
  2. All photos must appropriate.
  3. You don’t necessarily have to be in uniform, but it should be obvious which team you are with (i.e. a display of team colors, number, mascot, or some other indicator should be in the photo).
  4. Your photo should feature at least this year’s robot.
  5. Photos must be submitted by midnight on October 27th.
  6. To submit, upload your photo to Brunswick Eruption’s Facebook wall and include your team number in the post.
  7. Be creative! Remember, you’re not just preparing any old off-season—Brunswick Eruption is the ultimate November New Jersey Hawaiian robotics getaway!
Voting
  1. After being posted to the Brunswick Eruption Facebook wall, photos will be uploaded into the “BE 11 Photo Contest” album.
  2. To vote, simply “Like” a picture in the album.
  3. Voting will be open until 9AM on the morning of Brunswick Eruption 11 (November 3rd, 2012).
  4. The team whose photo receives the most votes will win 250 pts towards being in the running for the Aloha Award!

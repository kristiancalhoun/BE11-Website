How To Install

iPhone

  1. Click the icon, located at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Then select "Add to Home Screen."

Android

  1. Press the Menu button.
  2. Click "Bookmarks" and/or then click "Add Bookmark."
  3. From the bookmarks menu, long press the bookmark and click Add Shortcut to Homescreen.
      4. Alternately,
    1. Long press a blank area on the homescreen
    2. Select Shortcuts
    3. Select Bookmarks
    4. Choose the bookmark added for http://be.raiderrobotix.org/mobile.html.

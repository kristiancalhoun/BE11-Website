The Aloha Award is a fun, interactive scavenger hunt with a FIRST twist. Teams will be provided with a set of objectives and earn points for each task they complete. The team that earns the most points is the winner! The challenges are designed to allow teams to grow as a group and show their own team spirit by spreading the word of FIRST culture in their communities and at Brunswick Eruption.

This year's tasks are as follows:
  1. Decorate your pit with some Hawaiian flair. [100 pts]
  2. Dress your robot up for its Hawaiian Vacation to New Jersey. [100 pts]
  3. Dress team members in Hawaiian attire (skirts/shirts/be creative!). [10 pts/member]
  4. Bring your school’s principal or superintendent. [100 pts each]
  5. Bring the principal of a school who doesn’t have a FIRST team. Principals of pre-rookies qualify. [150 pts]
  6. Invite a government official (governor, senator, representative, delegate, etc.) [10 pts/printed e-mail or letter]
  7. Participate in the Evil Sundae contest [100 pts]
  8. Tweet why you love BE . Mention your team number and @BrunsEruption in the tweet. [25 pts per team + 5 additional points if you tweet us from your team’s official twitter]
  9. Alert the media! Get someone from the local media to show up and cover your team and BE [100 pts per outlet]
  10. Have your team submit an entry for the Mike Wade Memorial Award. [250 pts]
  11. Be in the top 3 for food donations. [150, 125, 100 pts]
  12. Be in the top 3 for shoe donations by weight. [150, 125, 100 pts]
  13. Have a team member or mentor attend a workshop at BE [75 pts]
  14. Bring a pre-rookie or lend a pre-rookie a robot. [250 pts]
  15. Bring an FTC team. [100 pts/team]
  16. Have team alumni volunteer at BE. [25 pts/alumnus]
  17. Decorate your car with your team number and the phrase “FIRST Robotics” and tweet a picture of it to @BrunsEruption. [100 pts/car]
  18. Enter the BE Facebook photo contest. [50 pts]
  19. Win the BE Facebook photo contest. [250 pts]
  20. Come in with something extra awesome and we will assign points as we see fit!
Rules
  1. All activities will be performed in a safe manner.
  2. Twitter feeds should be directed at the official event twitter (@BrunsEruption) and include the official event hashtag (#omgBE).
  3. Proof of all activities must be provided.
  4. If you are to bring prominent members such as the school principal, superintendent, Governor, or the President of the United States, please have students ready to escort them around and tell them about FIRST. Please also inform us by sending an email to brunswickeruption@gmail.com so we can prepare in advance.
  5. You are responsible for making sure that a judge is around when you are participating in an activity that generates points.
  6. In case of a tie, the team who completes the most challenges wins.
